Our Services

What we do

Axis3D offers reliable, FDM printing with attention to detail. With particular expertise in functional parts with real world and industrial uses. We print prototypes, replacement parts, and finished products in PLA and PETG.

We're not just another print farm, so you're not just another number.

All our parts are made to the highest quality, accurate to 100 microns† (thats 10 thousand times smaller than a meter).

Custom Prints

Send your STL or tell us what you need — we’ll print to spec and hand finish on request.

Prototyping

Fast iteration for design students, startups, and inventors. Short lead times and honest guidance.

Replacement Parts

Once-off, durable parts for home repairs, tools and appliances, measured to fit.

Small-batch Production

Custom parts for small-batch production runs. We’ll help you get your product to market.

Materials we stock

We primarily print in PLA and PETG. Other filaments available on request for specific use-cases.

PLA

PLA is the most common material for 3d printed parts. Its low cost, high versatility, and useful strength make it a top pick. Perfect for prototyping, decorative items, and most use cases. Also bio-degradeable.

Currently offered in:
• Galaxy multicoloured

€0.04 per gram

PET-G

Our top recommendation, PET-G is a middle ground between cost and usability. Perfect for functional parts its strong and still lightweight, able to resist impacts, and not brittle.

Currently offered in:
• White
• Metallic Grey
• Red

€0.065 per gram

Other Filiments

Although we recommended either PLA or PET-G for a fast turnaround, we may be able to print in other materials, just get in touch!
