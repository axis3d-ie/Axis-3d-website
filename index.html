Our Services

What we do

Axis3D offers reliable, FDM printing with attention to detail. With particular expertise in functional parts such as industrial machinery and feild-grade munition parts. We print prototypes, replacement parts, and finished products in PLA and PETG.

Custom Prints

Send your STL or tell us what you need — we’ll print to spec and finish on request.

Prototyping

Fast iteration for design students, startups, and inventors. Short lead times and honest guidance.

Replacement Parts

Small-batch, durable parts for home repairs, tools and appliances, measured to fit.

Materials we stock

We primarily print in PLA and PETG. Other filaments available on request for specific use-cases.

PLA

PLA is the most common material for 3d printed parts. Its low cost, high versatility, and useful strength make it a top pick. Perfect for prototyping, decorative items, and most use cases. Also bio-degradeable.

Currently offered in:
• Galaxy multicoloured

€0.04 per gram

PET-G

Our top recommendation, PET-G is a middle ground between cost and use ability. Perfect for functional parts its strong and still lightweight, able to resist impacts, and not brittle.

Currently offered in:
• White
• Metallic Grey
• Red

€0.065 per gram

Other Filiments

Although we recommended either PLA or PET-G for a fast turnaround, we may be able to print in other materials, just get in touch!
